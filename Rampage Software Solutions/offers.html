TYPES OF WEBSITES

In today's digital era, the internet is flooded with various types of websites already, and new ones are popping in every day in huge numbers. Picking the perfect website is extremely vital for you to reach your goals and maximize all the opportunities that the world wide web offers. Luckily we are here to help.
These are the most common types.

1. Social Media Websites
We are all aware of the giants Instagram and Facebook; who doesn't know of them? However, social media comes in various forms. These websites are normally made to let individuals share contemplations, pictures or thoughts, or just associate with others corresponding to a specific topic. Social media websites are progressively turning into the go-to goal for individuals to find out about the news, and connect with people.

2. E-Commerce Websites
The websites where users can shop and makes purchases are called E-Commerce Websites. They have somewhat become a must-have every selling business. As there are much more convenient for your customers, and also a lot more profitable than brick and motar. These sites have became very common

3. Portfolio Websites
Portfolio Websites are great for showcasing your work; everyone from photographers to models are utilising such sites to flaunt their work to potential clients. Essentially utilised by those in the creative business, a portfolio site can be utilised like a CV, showing your abilities so as to dazzle customers, clients, or future prospects.

4. Blog Websites
Blogs have been a most loved and preferred choice of websites for individuals and families who wish to archive the huge events of their lives, for example, weddings, babies or their precious moments. Blogs are also great for presenting your personal style, sharing your favourite recipes or showcasing your hobbies or anything in that case to the world.

5. Business/Corporate Websites
A business site is any site that is committed to depicting a particular business. It ought to be branded like the company (a similar logo and positioning) and convey the sorts of items as well as services the company/ business offers. At this point, each company out there, whether big or small, ought to have a site. Each potential client you come across will simply expect that in the event that they Google your business searching for more data, they'll discover a site. What's more, in case they don't, it makes the business look less professional or legitimate.

With the above mentions types of websites, we hope that you would've gained vivid information about the same, which would help you choose the right one for your business.
